School Application Project

PRAYAS is working on creating an application for the school. Nowadays, everything is running towards to be digital, which makes things easier and transparent. This application will make functioning of school pretty easier and also will help in storing the data of students in very arranged manner so that can be easily accessible by the students and school directly without much effort. It will also be a very reliable platform for students. There will be separate log-ins for administration, teachers and students. They can easily go through their past records, current activities and future plans. This also have feature like recorded lectures, notes, content, fee details, time table and bus tracing for administrative. It will also help in learning modern skills like communication and coding which is not available in so many schools because of lack of knowledge in this fields in rural areas.Within this application, we will also add additional and trending features like coding and student based activities that can be conducted within the classroom. We plan on creating customized packages that can be used by different educational institutions across the country to shift to a digitalized mode. This can help them in developing an application with features according to their needs.

Innovative Education Council(IEC)

Innovative Education Council(IEC) have a vision of reforming our education system by shifting it towards an organic learning approach and ensuring holistic development of every child. Organic learning is learning that occurs in a natural state. It is learning that develops from within a child (or adult). The child develops curiosity about a topic,concept, problem, or idea, then seeks out methods to satisfy this curiosity. The current senerio is, Indiaâ€™s learning crisis is not new. Overthe previous years, the percentafe of students enrolled in schools has increased but are they really learning? Many national surveys reveal that a large number of students still struggle to do basic mathematical operations and read/write sentences in English. Moreover, the current teaching methods fails to spark curiosity in the students mind and help them translate their learnings to real world. Our Approch is Children grow and learn by being active and involved. They learn by playing, interacting with others and learning by doing. We aim to use these natural play and learn process of children to teach them maths in the existing curriculum for classes 1-5. Moreover, we have developed a list of 100 activities to conduct in classes Nursery to 5, which are essential for holistic and overall development of a child. We provide booklets and kits, training to teachers to help them seamlessly transform the daily classes into fun and interactive learning sessions and continuous evaluation for improvement of our methods.
